--- title: p2pool now supports terracoin time: 08:14:36 date: December 08, 2012 category: news summary: Terracoin network parameters were added to p2pool. --- we submitted a patch to [p2pool](https://en.bitcoin.it/wiki/P2Pool) to provide terracoin support. Thanks to p2pool author, those modifications were quickly merged into [p2pool official sourcecode](https://github.com/forrestv/p2pool). This gives you another pool-mining environment, so you can generate terracoins using a decentralized, DoS-resistant mining pool. Our support pages should soon provide detailed instructions, until then, here's a quick [p2pool howto](/p2pool_howto.txt).
