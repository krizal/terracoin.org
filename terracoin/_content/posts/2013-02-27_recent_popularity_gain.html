--- title: Recent popularity gain time: 11:14:36 date: February 27, 2013 category: news summary: News from the blockchain --- The first terracoin blockchain entry was created on October 26th 2012 and the first major user spike occured on November 1st, following various discussion forum announcements. With more user-created content and services coming to life, the terracoin network got more popular over last weeks, totalizing more than 3350 distinct participant machines since launch, more than 150000 transactions, for a total of 1.6 Millions terracoins. Network regularly crosses over the rate of 10 transactions per minute, total computing power put into transaction validation and the various terracoin-enabled services coming to life shows quite some interest in terracoins.
