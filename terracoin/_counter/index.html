Terracoin

Peer to peer decentralized digital currency with no central authority, no chargeback and low transaction fees.

Mandatory build warning

Terracoin client version 0.8.0.2 was made mandatory on October 3rd 2013. Please upgrade your terracoin client before sending any payment.

Highlights

worldwide coverage:
take a few seconds to generate a receiving address using your terracoin client and you are ready to accept payments from any country.
opensource:
no hidden surprise, anyone may audit the full sourcecode at any time.
community-driven:
based on the bitcoin currency, terracoin regularly include updates from both communities contributors.
