Recent popularity gain

February 27, 2013 | news

The first terracoin blockchain entry was created on October 26th 2012 and the first major user spike occured on November 1st, following various discussion forum announcements.

With more user-created content and services coming to life, the terracoin network got more popular over last weeks, totalizing more than 3350 distinct participant machines since launch, more than 150000 transactions, for a total of 1.6 Millions terracoins.

Network regularly crosses over the rate of 10 transactions per minute, total computing power put into transaction validation and the various terracoin-enabled services coming to life shows quite some interest in terracoins.

Stable build 0.1.2-23

February 12, 2013 | news

New terracoin stable release 0.1.2-23 is now available, pre-built windows binaries are available sourceforge.

This build includes a lot of performance improvements, along with a set of new features.

New rpc commands are now available, to lock/unlock unspent “coins”, importprivkey rpc command was also enhanced to enable/disable blockchain rescan, terracoin transaction notifier is now part of stable builds.
